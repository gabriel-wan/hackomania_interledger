Welcome to Community Fund

A community-powered emergency fund built on Interledger. Contributions are pooled together and automatically distributed to members affected by natural disasters.

💚

I Want to Donate

Contribute funds via your Interledger testnet wallet. Your donation goes directly into the community pool and is distributed to disaster-affected members automatically.

🙋

I Need Emergency Aid

Register as a member with your wallet address and location. When a disaster is detected near you, you'll automatically receive a payout from the fund — no application needed.

How It Works

1

Contribute

Donors send funds via Interledger. Payments are approved in your wallet.

2

Pool Funds

All contributions are pooled into a transparent community fund.

3

Detect Disaster

USGS & NWS APIs are polled for earthquakes and severe weather alerts.

4

Auto Payout

Members near the disaster epicenter receive payouts automatically via Interledger.
